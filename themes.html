Key Themes

  • Survival
  • Oppression
  • Freedom
  • Power and Control
  • Sacrifice

Analysis

The Hunger Games explores these themes through its characters' struggles in a society marked by inequality and brutality. It challenges readers to reflect on the consequences of power imbalances and the ethical dilemmas faced by individuals in the face of oppression.
