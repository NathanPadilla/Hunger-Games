Set in a dystopian future, The Hunger Games trilogy follows the story of Katniss Everdeen, a young girl living in the oppressive society of Panem. The annual Hunger Games, a brutal event where teenagers are forced to fight to the death on live television, serve as a reminder of the Capitol's power and control over the districts.

As you navigate through the pages of this website, you'll discover the rich characters, intricate plot, and thought-provoking themes that make The Hunger Games an unforgettable literary experience.
