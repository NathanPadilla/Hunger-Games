Overview

The Hunger Games trilogy is set in a dystopian future where the totalitarian nation of Panem is divided into 12 districts and the Capitol. Each year, the Capitol selects one boy and one girl, known as tributes, from each district to compete in the Hunger Games, a televised event where the tributes fight to the death until only one remains.
